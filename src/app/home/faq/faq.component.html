What are some tips for good images?

Make sure to include their full name when generating for best results! EX: Sonic -> 'Sonic the hedgehog', Tails -> 'Tails the fox'.

The model was trained on booru tags, using them allows your images to turn out better and be more specific. If you would like to turn an existing image into booru tags to input into the model, use the following website: https://huggingface.co/spaces/Metal079/wd-v1-4-tags and copy the output into the prompt.

Placing parenthesis (around) one or ((several tags)) will give that tag more emphasis and increase the likelihood that it is included in the prompt. The more (((parenthesis))) the more emphasis. Ex: "Amy Rose ((wearing a pokadot dress))" Note: Too much emphasis can degrade image quality, dont go overboard. You can also use syntax like (parenthesis:1.3) which is equivalent to (((parenthesis))) to reduce the amount of parenthesis needed.

About

Hi there! I'm a big fan of AI and Sonic, so I thought why not combine the two? As someone who never had the mind to express themselves artistically, AI art has opened up a whole new world for me and I'd like to be able to share that openly for every sonic fan to express themselves!

I'm hoping to be able to keep this site up as long as I'm financially able to, but as the site grows more popular it will be hard to keep up with GPU demand and server cost, so any support is greatly appreciated!

AI Disclaimer

Remember who made the images! AI art is an amazing tool that lets everyone create art, but it's important to remember that using the AI does not make one an artist. Please be respectful of the artists who made the images that made the AI you use possible, and do not claim any of the images you create as your own. That doesnt mean you shouldnt be able to use the AI for ideas and inspiration for your own art though! AI is a tool for everyone to use and enjoy!
